Shiba Inu

History

They are from Japan and they were bred for hunting. The are cute and yes they are the Doge from the memes. The first Shiba Inus that came to the USA was in 1954 by armed services.

Size and more

When they are born then can weigh up to 3 pounds

The colors they come in are,

  • Red
  • Cream
  • Tan
  • Black

Care

They need to be walked at least twice a day and also they have a dense layer of fur. In the winter or summer they shed and will lose fur. This is a big probaly because they need their fur to help them live in the heat or cold.
